High Velocity Aerial Filming

Aerial Filming took a giant leap forward when Blue Sky and Peter Degerfeldt challenged Gyro-stabilized Systems in Grass Valley, US, to build a GSS 520 5 axis system that would produce rock solid images at a speed of more than 300 knots per hour.
